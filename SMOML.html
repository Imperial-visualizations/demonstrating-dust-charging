DRAFT/COPIED - Shifted Modified Orbital Limited Motion - SMOML

SOML can be easily extended for large grains if electron flows are neglected. The electron current is again just the usual OML expression. However, the SMOML ion current is ob-tained by applying SOML to the sheath edge in the same way as in section 2.2.4 . This produces a floating condition: $$ exp(-\Phi^f_d) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu \theta}}[\theta \eta_1(U_i)+\frac{\eta_2(U_i)}{\theta}(\Phi^f_d -\frac{1}{2}ln[\frac{2\pi}{\mu}(1+\gamma\theta)])] $$ re-write in lambert function form and solve, reduces to previous solutions for $U_i$ tends to zero.
